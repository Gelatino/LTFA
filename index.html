ABOUT

LTFA aims to give a stage to films that deserve more eyes. Each edition centers on independent cinema and unfolds into a day of films, music, conversation, and drinks.

It’s not just about watching a film — it’s about the people, the music, the drinks, the new friends, the talking, the images...

and all the eyes.
