ABOUT

THE LTL AFFAIR IS A FILM CIRCULATION PROJECT THAT TAKES FOR EACH EDITION A NEW INDEPENDENT FILMMAKER’S WORK AND WEAVES AROUND IT A FULL DAY PROGRAM OF FABULOUS AND RESONANTING FILM WORKS FROM OTHER ARTISTS. THE IDEA IS TO FIND NEW CONNECTIONS IN A MULTIPILICITY OF FILM PRACTICES, NEW OLD AND SUPPORT THEIR CIRCULATION.
